All about Morgan Heinemann

Morgan was born in Los Angeles in early 1984 but moved to the town of Bellingham, WA in 1989 with his parents, grandmother, dog 'Noodles', and his cat Willy.

Between the ages of 5 and 18 Morgan attended 9 different schools, up to 3 in the same year of middle school. Morgan graduated Washington State University with a business degree before taking a teaching job in Japan, where he lived for the next 20 months.

After returning to the states he worked a number of odd jobs until taking employment at a Telematics company in the customer service department before taking on further positions as a technical trainer, troubleshooter, and eventually software tester which he then did for the next 6 years while also discovering his love for Dungeons and Dragons.

Leaving his testing job for a software consultancy position he delved further into software development, traveling Australia, and welcoming his first born son Mordecai in to the world. As that career became less tenable he chose to leave and attend Codefellows full time in hopes of honing his skills as a software developer to better provide for his family and spend time with his son, wife, cat, and giant dog.
